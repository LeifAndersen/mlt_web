--- layout: post title: Synfig Studio uses MLT for cross-platform sound category: blog --- Synfig Studio is a good, free, open source, cross-platform 2D animation program. They wanted to add audio support, but they needed more than just a hardware abstraction layer for audio output. They needed more than a multi-format/-codec library. They also wanted something that provides timing and mixing with a succinct API. They chose MLT.
